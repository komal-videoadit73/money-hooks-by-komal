About Komal

The mentor behind every hook to smarter money

Komal is the founder of Money Hooks — a personal brand built around one simple idea: money habits shouldn't feel complicated, and they definitely shouldn't feel boring.

Through short, punchy educational videos, Komal teaches kids, students, families and adults practical ways to save, budget and shop smarter — turning everyday decisions into lifelong habits.

No jargon. No lectures. Just simple "hooks" that make saving money click.
